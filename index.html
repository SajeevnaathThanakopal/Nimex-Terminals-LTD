About Us

Nimex Terminals — Abu Dhabi’s Gateway to the Future of Energy

Nimex Terminals — based in Abu Dhabi, we are pioneering LNG & LPG storage, handling, and shipment infrastructure that drives the UAE’s energy vision. Our world-class terminals deliver safe, efficient, and high-capacity storage, regasification, bottling, and loading/unloading operations, ensuring seamless energy supply chains and connecting the region to global markets. Through innovation, strategic partnerships, and operational excellence, Nimex Terminals is shaping the future of LNG & LPG terminal operations, logistics, and distribution across the Middle East and beyond.
